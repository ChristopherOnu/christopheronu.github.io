Planet Grant — Automated Grant Management System

Make.comWorkflow AutomationNonprofitOperations
Built a complete end-to-end grant management system for Planet Grant that automates the full applicant journey from public intake through eligibility checking, internal review, and final decision notifications. Using Jotform, Make.com, Airtable, Gmail, and Slack, the system replaced a manual grants process with a structured, automated pipeline that keeps applicants informed and the internal team organised at every stage.
The Problem

Planet Grant was running a grant program without a proper operational system behind it. Applications were coming in with no automated way to check eligibility, store records cleanly, route applicants through a review process, or send consistent communications based on where each applicant stood. The team was handling intake, follow-up, and decision emails manually, which created delays, inconsistency, and significant administrative overhead for a small team managing a high-stakes program.

The Solution

Designed and built a multi-tool automation workflow that handles the entire grants pipeline without manual intervention at routine stages. A public Jotform intake form captures initial submissions and feeds records directly into Airtable, where eligibility is automatically checked against admin-defined criteria. Eligible applicants receive a conditional email inviting them to complete a full application, while ineligible applicants receive a clear decline notification. Accepted full applications are pushed into the review pipeline in Airtable where board members can assign scores. Based on the outcome, the system automatically triggers onboarding or decline emails through Gmail and fires Slack notifications to keep the internal team updated in real time throughout the process.

Key Features
  • Public Jotform intake form capturing initial applicant submissions and feeding records directly into Airtable
  • Automated eligibility checking against admin-defined rules including region and budget criteria, running immediately on each new submission
  • Conditional email responses through Gmail sending eligible applicants an invitation to the full application and ineligible applicants a decline notification
  • Full application stage for selected applicants with records stored and organised in Airtable for internal review
  • Board member scoring tracked within Airtable across the review pipeline
  • Automated onboarding or decline emails triggered by final status decisions without manual sending
  • Slack notifications keeping the internal team informed of key pipeline updates and status changes in real time
  • Make.com automations connecting every tool and handling all conditional logic, data routing, and trigger management across the workflow
Tools Used
Jotform Make.com Airtable Gmail Slack
Results & Impact

Planet Grant moved from a manually managed grants process to a structured automated pipeline that handles applicant communication, eligibility decisions, and internal review coordination without the team needing to intervene at every step. Applicants receive fast, professional responses at each stage rather than waiting on someone to send an email. The internal team gets Slack updates as applications move through the pipeline, so everyone stays informed without chasing status updates. The system is built cleanly in Airtable and Make.com, making it straightforward for a small team to maintain and expand as the program grows.
