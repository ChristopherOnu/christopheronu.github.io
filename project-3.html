KAY Property — Operations System

Monday.comWorkflow AutomationReal EstateOperations
Built a fully structured Monday.com operations system for KAY Property, a buyer's advocacy business operating across two arms: local purchases in Geelong and Melbourne, and interstate investment purchases. The build consolidated client-facing property tracking, internal process management, and investment market research into a set of purpose-built boards that the team can maintain and update without any technical overhead.
The Problem

KAY Property was already using Monday.com but their board structure had grown without clear design, making it hard for the team to move fast and hard for clients to follow along. They needed three distinct operational layers working together: a client-facing view of properties being tracked, an internal checklist covering every step from onboarding to post-settlement, and a research board for storing and updating investment market data across states and cities. Without the right structure, columns, and automations in place, the team was doing a lot of manual work to keep things consistent.

The Solution

Redesigned and built out the full Monday.com workspace from scratch with a clean, standardised board structure across all three layers. Client Property Observation Boards were built for both business arms, giving clients a clear, professional view of every property being tracked on their behalf. Internal Client Process Checklist Boards were built separately for each arm, mapping every step from terms of engagement through to post-settlement gifting. The investment arm's checklist included additional steps specific to interstate acquisitions. The Investment Insights Board was set up as a structured research database covering key market metrics across states, cities, and local government areas. Correct column types were applied across every board, status workflows were standardised, and automations were configured to reduce manual follow-up for the team.

Key Features
  • Client-facing Property Observation Boards for both business arms, tracking campaign type, price range, auction dates, rental yield, real estate links, documents, status labels, and media
  • Internal Process Checklist Boards tracking every milestone from onboarding through to post-settlement, with investment-specific steps added for the interstate arm including suburb reports, investment strategy documents, and rental appraisals
  • Investment Insights Board storing market research data including vacancy rates, rental yields, 10-year capital growth, affordability index, days on market, incoming supply, demographics, and IRSAD scores
  • Standardised column types across all boards covering numbers, dates, status dropdowns, file uploads, and URL links for data accuracy
  • Consistent status workflows built across boards so the team always knows where each client or property stands
  • Automations configured to trigger reminders and notifications on status changes and key milestone completions
  • Loom walkthrough documenting the full setup and how to use each board

Results & Impact

KAY Property went from a Monday.com setup that had outgrown itself to a clean, intentional system built around how their business actually operates. Clients now have a professional, easy-to-read view of their property search without needing to chase the team for updates. Internally, nothing gets missed across a long settlement process because every step is mapped and tracked. The investment research board gives the team a single place to store and reference market data rather than digging through spreadsheets or documents. The whole system was handed over with documentation so the team can maintain it independently going forward.
